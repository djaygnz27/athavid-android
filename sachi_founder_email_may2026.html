📦 What Happened — The Migration

Over the past few days, we completed a major upgrade to the Sachi platform. We migrated our entire database and backend infrastructure to a new, faster, more scalable system built for long-term growth.

During this migration, some of you may have noticed that your videos disappeared, likes reset to zero, or comment counts showed blank. We know how frustrating that is — your content matters and it should be visible. We're deeply sorry for the disruption.

🔧 What We Fixed — Day 1 (May 19)

✅ Video feed restored

All 221 videos were re-linked to their correct creators. Videos that had gone missing from profiles were found and restored.
✅ Likes and comments backfilled

Over 480 likes and 192 comments that were wiped during migration were recovered and re-synced to every video.
✅ Profile videos reappeared

86 video records that were attributed to old user IDs were migrated to your new Sachi accounts so your profile content is back.
✅ TikTok-style autoplay fixed

Scrolling the feed now auto-plays videos as you swipe, just like it should — including on your own profile page.
✅ Upload errors resolved

A schema validation error that blocked new video and photo uploads was identified and fixed. Posting now works cleanly again.
✅ Login loop fixed

Some users were getting stuck in a birthday or sign-in loop every time they opened the app. This was caused by a mismatch in the user database and has been permanently resolved.

🔧 What We Fixed — Day 2 (May 20)

✅ Heart (❤️) button fixed

The like button was using a stale in-memory count that could drift when multiple users liked at the same time. It now fetches the live count from the database before saving — fully accurate.
✅ Comment counts now stay accurate

Feed cards were showing 0 comments even after people had commented. We fixed the count to refresh from the database every time you open the comment section, and every 90 seconds automatically in the background.
✅ Music picker audio fixed

When selecting a background track in the music picker, the original video audio was not muting properly. Fixed — the selected song now plays cleanly.
✅ Live engagement counts refresh silently

Likes, comments, views, and shares now quietly update every 90 seconds in the background — no reload, no scroll interruption. What you see in the feed is always current.
