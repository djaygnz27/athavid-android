## Full Description (4000 characters max)

🎬 **Sachi — Your Stage. Your World.**

Sachi is the short video platform built for real creators. Whether you're dancing, cooking, cracking jokes, or sharing life's unscripted moments — Sachi gives you the stage.

**🔥 Why Sachi?**

• **Vertical short videos** — Full-screen format built for mobile-first creators
• **Smooth feed** — Swipe through an infinite scroll of trending content
• **Like, comment & share** — Engage with creators you love
• **Upload instantly** — Record and post in seconds, directly from your phone
• **Discover what's trending** — Explore hashtags and go viral

**🎯 Built for Creators**

Sachi is designed for people who want to be seen. Your content takes center stage — no clutter, no distractions. Just you and your audience.

**👥 Community First**

Follow creators, build your audience, and become a part of the next generation of short-form storytelling. Sachi is where the next wave of viral content begins.

**📱 Designed for Mobile**

Silky-smooth vertical video playback, optimized for every Android device. Fast loads, clean UI, and a feed that keeps you coming back.

**✅ Safe & Moderated**

Sachi has built-in AI-assisted content moderation and a verification system to keep the platform safe and enjoyable for everyone.

**🚀 Join the Beta**

Sachi is currently in beta — be among the first creators on the platform. Early users shape the community, the features, and the culture.

Download now and claim your stage. 🌟
